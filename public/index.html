Resource to estimate the calorie needs of endurance athletes.

Know your resting metabolic rate (rmr)

Resting metabolic rate is the amount of calories per day your body uses to keep you alive. If you have a desk job you can multiply this number by 1.2 to get an idea of how many calories you burn without exercise.

Know your macro nutrient needs

The intensity of your workouts affects the nutrients your body needs to recover properly. If your workout was only a moderate intensity your upper and lower protein and fat requirments will be the same. This represents the maximum you should consume on that day.

Calculate calories burned during exercise

Using your weight, exercise type, duration and the average speed of your exercise you will be provided with an estimate of the calories you used.

Track your daily needs

Use this tool daily to see your daily calorie requirments. After some time you will understand how to best eat to recover from different types of workouts.
